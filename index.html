Titli

Titli is a social entrepreneurship model which entails women as the primary stakeholder. Women are undoubtedly an integral part of any economy, and the menstrual cycle is a crucial biological aspect of their lives.However in India, discussions about menstruation is a social taboo and “Titli” works precisely on tackling this problem.Titli solves this problem by distributing machine manufactured sterilised sanitary pads across different parts of the country to women,especially in the rural areas.
